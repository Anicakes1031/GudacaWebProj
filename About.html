About us!

Gudaca is committed to fulfil its corporate objective of providing quality chicken products to its various customers at reasonable prices. As such, it continues to seek representation with reputable dressing plant in the industry. Backed by almost 10 years expertise of its owner, Arnel E. Gudaca, the company is positioned to be one of the industry’s leading distributors of chicken products. We are confident that we will be able to serve your prestigious company’s requirements, and we look forward to become one of your company’s accredited suppliers.
